About Us

The Legendary Bushwood Golf Course

Nestled in the heart of Davie, Florida, Bushwood Golf Course is a golfer's paradise that offers an unparalleled blend of award-winning courses and breathtaking vistas. Our meticulously designed fairways and greens have earned Bushwood numerous accolades, solidifying our reputation as one of the premier golf destinations in South Florida.

Immerse yourself in the beauty of the lush, manicured landscapes as you tee off on our pristine courses. With undulating hills, strategically placed hazards, and stunning water features, every round of golf here is an unforgettable experience. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a novice, our courses cater to all skill levels, ensuring an enjoyable and challenging game for everyone.
